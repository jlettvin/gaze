This model simplifies neurons and muscles, but not to the point of unnatural. 292 twitch units are shown in a single muscle fiber bundle.

Twitch units generate a shortening force. The visualization below shows a cross-section of the muscle where each square is a model of a muscle fiber's cut end.

The model shows twitching muscle fibers as light red. Muscle fibers at rest or recovering are shown in dark red.

Muscles are composed from hierarchical bundles of muscle fibers. Twitch units are single muscle fibers running end-to-end and excited by a single motor neuron.

A motor neuron attaches at many points along a single twitch unit. A twitch signal is sent simultaneously to all neuromuscular junctions along a single muscle fiber.

The shape of the remote dendrite for the neuron feeding a muscle fiber has a complex shape implementing a sampler of a regular sweeping pattern. The dendrite hosts a fixed set of pre-randomized dendritic spines. As the pattern passes across the dendritic spines those which sample a boundary in the pattern twitch their muscle fibers.

The remote dendrite for each motor neuron expresses a pattern which makes its gradient detectors unique within the sampled pattern. It most likely samples the pattern in multiple places and uses the coincidence of successful samples to trigger the pulse to activate a muscle fiber.

As long as the pattern delivered is reliable, twitch unit duty cycle timing requirements are satisfied. This strategy guarantees maximum recovery time for all muscle fibers.

The illustration below shows this behavior. The code driving this image is logically equivalent to the proposed pre-randomized sampling of a regular pattern. Note the (0,0) coordinates at the center of the muscle.

Twitch patterns show a repeating weak/STRONG tonus for 1 second each. This simple demonstration illustrates a logical economy enabling implementations to ignore parallelism and simply act concurrently on all actual delivered pulses, whether they are dense or sparse, without the burden of unnecessary calculation.

This is one of the economies expected in vivo.
